Ron Burgundy

Five-time (local) Emmy award-winning journalist, Ron Burgundy dominated the San Diego news racket from the late 60’s to the early 70’s. Although he was most notable for his amazing abilities to deliver the news, he was also a man of deep passion, loved a good glass of Scotch and was not shy about showing off his exceptional “guns”.

Yes, it’s true, Ron Burgundy lived a life that most men can only dream of. He had the touch with the ladies, and broke many hearts along the way. But as the story goes, along came a woman who would forever change his life. Veronica Corningstone and Ron fell in love and eventually co-anchored for CNN. They spent many years side by side reporting the worlds news before retiring and traveling abroad. To this day, there has never been another man quite like Ron Burgundy.

You will forever be in our hearts Ron....stay classy.
